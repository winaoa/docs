5. Seepage

Due to upstairs flat:
Seepage from an upstairs flat is the responsibility of that flat’s owner (bathroom, kitchen, or any other modification). RWA’s plumber staff will help identify the root cause. Any additional maintenance work will be charged as per the Plumbing Work section above.

Due to supply pipe in the block / water tank on the roof:
RWA conducts periodic surveys to find leakages, but all block members should also report issues to the maintenance helpdesk. RWA will supply labor to fix main tank or pipe issues; material costs will be borne equally by all 16 flat owners. Early identification and repair of seepage are essential to minimize damage.
