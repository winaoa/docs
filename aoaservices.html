RWA Office Services to Residents

When You Buy a Flat

  • Obtain NOC from Society: Pay 0.5% + GST of the flat purchase price (Sale Agreement or registry) as per UP Apartment Act.
  • Get AOA membership:
    • Fill and submit Form B along with:
      • First three pages of registry (front & back).
      • One photograph.
      • Membership fees of ₹1,000 by cheque or cash (collect receipt).

When You Rent an Apartment

  • Collect Tenant Entry Form from office.
  • Pay tenant entry fees of ₹1,500 + GST.
  • Submit:
    • Copy of rent agreement.
    • Copy of Aadhar card.
    • Student or work ID (if applicable).
  • Police verification mandatory (with dealer stamp if applicable).
  • Bachelor tenants must submit an affidavit on stamp paper stating they will not create nuisance; RWA may terminate agreement or enforce vacation if violated.

Somebody Leaving the Society

  • If a renter vacates the property:
    • Obtain NOC from maintenance office (clear all dues).
    • Submit NOC form to RWA office with Aadhar copy.
  • If owner or relatives vacate:
    • Clear maintenance dues and get NOC from maintenance team.
    • Send email to winaoa@gmail.com.

Work Permit in the Flat

  • Collect Work Permit & Contractor forms from society office.
  • Deposit security money:
    • ₹10,000 for ground-floor civil work.
    • ₹2,000 for work on other floors.
  • Submit for all labourers & contractors:
    • Copy of Aadhar card.
    • Two photographs each.
  • Gate pass fees: ₹100 + GST per worker/contractor.
  • Contractor must maintain police verification records for all labourers and submit to office.

Security Refund

  • Security deposit refund is subject to:
    • Work done as per approved permit.
    • Manager’s site survey confirmation.
    • Submission of refund application with original receipt.
  • Refunds issued by cheque for amounts ≥ ₹10,000; smaller refunds may be processed in cash at resident’s convenience.

Malva Removal

  • Malva removal is owner’s responsibility.
  • Failure to remove leads to a charge of ₹500 + GST per tractor.

Work Permit in the Block Building

  • Obtain signatures from at least eight residents in the building.
  • Submit signed application to RWA office.

Maid Pass

  • Maid pass issued only upon submission of valid police verification.
