Q&A

Q1. Why can't we use the sinking fund to cover monthly expenses?
A: The sinking fund is legally and financially reserved for large infrastructure repairs only.
Q2. What happens after the ₹54 lakh reserve is used up?
A: We may face service disruptions or need to urgently raise maintenance unless corrective action is taken now.
Q3. How long can we continue current spending?
A: At the current rate, the reserve can support expenses for 8–10 months.
Q4. Can maintenance charges be revised without consensus?
A: Any revision would follow discussion in GBMs and approval by majority.
